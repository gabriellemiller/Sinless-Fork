EVERYTHING BUT THE DIET

I want to use this space to talk about all things that have to do with having a balanced and holistic lifestyle.

Why Sleep is So Important

Although I don't practice this as often as I should, sleeping is vital for overall health. One thing is getting sleep, but getting restful sleep is a whoooole other thing. I have always stayed away from sleeping medications because I believe natural remedies work better in the long run. I also try to not use my phone an hour before bed, read a little to get my sleepy mode on, and cool down my sleeping area to make my sheets feel a little more cozy. Some natural remedies that I use are: lavender spray, chamomile tea, and a weighted blanket.

My Instant Mood Boosters

Sometimes we have happy days, and sometimes we have stressful days where we wish time would go slow. I have found that the simplest things can turn our moods upside down in a matter of no time.

Play some music to wind down and de-stress

There is no thing that makes me happier than getting in the car alone, going on a drive, and blasting music till you can sing and can't hear your voice. I have linked my happy playlist here and hope you can take a chance to listen to it on your next drive!

Connect with Nature

Sometimes all we need to de-stress from a bad day is to disconnect from our phones and our overwhelming social media feeds and just need to go outside and breathe natural air to reconnect with ourselves.

Next time you're feeling a down or are looking for a little pick me up, I invite you to try these out. Above all, always remember that it's okay to have a bad day and that the bad days are what make us appreciate the good ones.
