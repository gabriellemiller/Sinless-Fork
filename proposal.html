Welcome to the Sinless Fork!

What is your website about?

My website is going to be about forgetting about all the fad diets, quick fixes, and past false beliefs of how to be "skinny and healthy". I plan to promote what it means to live a healthy lifestyle for YOU. Not everyones health journey is the same, but it should always be one that is accepting and guiltless. It's okay to have days where you don't feel like working out, it's okay to have other days where you feel you can workout twice a day. It is also okay to have days where you want to satisfy those cravings. I plan to promote a way of living that is doable and approachable. Anyone of any age can start, it is never too late!

What is the objective of your website?

I believe many blogs promote "quick fix" diet and the perfect lifestyle that one should live. I want to be raw and honest in my website. I want to provide healthy recipes, non-healthy recipes, workout routines that I enjoy, healthy lifestyle tips, and more! The point is: I want to communicate to my audience that there is no finish line towards living a healthy lifestyle. Of course we could create obtainable goals along the way but perfection is impossible to reach. I want people to be more informed of what it means to live holistically and how one small change in your everyday routine could turn into life changing decisions.

Who is your target audience set?

I plan my target audience to be young females, ages 18-25, who are currently under the constant pressures of social media and having to look a certain way. However, I believe my content will be useful to both men and women of all ages.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

I plan to make this website "blog-style" so I want to talk about my personal health journey. I've been through ups and downs and I want to dedicate a page about why I want to share my thoughts and what got me to where I am today. I also want to provide recipes to foods I eat daily. Recipes that satisfy my sweet and salty cravings and one that don't necessarily focus on the nutritional value but the joy that it brings me when I eat it. I want to provide health tips that I have implemented to my everyday routine (i.e. the importance of planning meals ahead, scheduling your day before hand, adopting hobbies that better you mentally, physically, and emotionally), I also want to add a page of clothes that I believe are very comfortable to use during my workouts, my grocery shopping lists in order to help my stay healthy, and lastly workout routines that I normally do. I am not quite sure if I want to make it Miami-focused, which is where I am from, but maybe I will provide healthy food places that I enjoy eating at and local markets that I purchase grocery items from.
